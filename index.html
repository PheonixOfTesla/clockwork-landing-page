Verify Your Download

Every release is cryptographically signed. Verify the SHA-256 checksum:

# Download checksum file
curl -O https://github.com/PheonixOfTesla/omniverse-core/releases/latest/download/checksums.sha256

# Verify your download
sha256sum -c checksums.sha256
